How to use the Neck & Décolletage LED

Step 1:

Cleanse and dry your skin to prepare it for ultimate LED light absorption. Secure the device around your neck using the adjustable velcro straps.

Step 2:

Step 3:

The device will automatically shut off after 10 minutes. Remove the device and gently wipe it down. Follow up with your normal skincare routine. That’s it!

The answers to your questions

Two clinically proven wavelengths of light (red 633nm and near-infrared 830nm) work on a cellular level to stimulate new collagen, reduce fine lines and wrinkles, and reduce sun damage and the visible signs of aging.

LEDs: 55 Dual-core LEDs
Wavelengths: 630 nm, 830 (Red and InfraRed)

For best results, we recommend using the device 5-7 times a week. Each session only lasts 10 minutes. Visible results may vary, but improvements can be seen within 4 weeks of consistent use. More significant changes can be seen over several months. Be sure to take before and after pictures to track your progress. Sometimes it can be hard to notice a difference until you compare your photos. Just remember, consistency is key!

Just 10 minutes! A one and a half hour charge can deliver up to 7 to 8 treatments

Yes, this device is safe for all skin types. It is non-invasive and gentle on the skin. However, if you have specific skin conditions, we recommend consulting with a dermatologist before use.

While it's primarily designed for the neck and chest, some users apply it to their face or other body areas such as the back and hands.. Refer to the user manual for specific usage recommendations.

For optimal results use the device on clean, bare skin. Creating a barrier between the device and your skin with unauthorized skincare products may diminish its effectiveness or potentially cause skin reactions.

Pregnant and breastfeeding individuals should exercise caution when considering any new skincare or beauty treatment. It is generally recommended to consult with a healthcare professional, before using such devices during pregnancy or while breastfeeding.

To clean the Qure Neck & Décolletage LED follow these steps: turn it off, gently wipe the treatment head with a damp cloth, avoid harsh chemicals, dry it thoroughly, and store it in a cool, dry place

Charge Neck & Décolletage device fully for 4 hours before your first use. Only use a 5V adapter or computer USB port. Please avoid fast chargers as they can damage the battery.

To charge the device, insert the USB-C cable into the controller. Insert the USB-A side of the cable into a USB power supply. When the controller is charged, the LED indicator on the controller flashes white, and when the controller is fully charged, the indicator will be steady white. Before charging, make sure that the plug and socket are completely dry. Failure to do so may result in electric shock, short circuit, or fire. Always unplug the device before use, the device will not work while being charged.
