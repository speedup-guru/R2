Micro-infusion System

When and what kind of results can I expect with Micro-Infusion?

Youll likely notice a radiance boost, softer fine lines and overall a healthy fresh complexion in the next day or two.

The more treatments you perform the greater skin benefits over time youll receive. This quick and easy home facial is perfect to perform a day or two before a big event, when you need to look to boost your skins complexion, fast.

How often can I do the treatment?

We recommend a Micro-Infusion treatment every 2 weeks for the best results!

Is it painful? Does it hurt?

Although the 0.5mm needles are longer than most at-home needling systems (making it more effective), the ultra-fine needles are thinner than a human hair, so that means youll feel minimal to no pain during treatment.

During the initial stages of treatment, you may experience a prickling sensation as the needles make contact with the skin but youll get used to the feeling quickly.

How many treatments do I get out of the Micro-Infusion 3 Month Bundle?

Total of 6 treatments. Each needle head is to be used one time per treatment, and disposed of after each treatment. This minimizes the risk of infection and unnecessary damage to the skin so you can get the best results. Use 1 full serum ampoule per treatment.

What serums come in the bundle? Can I use any serum with my Micro-Infusion device?

We recommend only using the serums that come in the bundle. Some serums may cause irritation and should be avoided! We have developed our potent serum ampoules specifically for Qures Micro-Infusion system, with low risk of irritation and with no fragrance.

There are two types of serums that come in the bundle, you can mix them together to experience a complete set of benefits that treats your specific skin concerns.

Rejuvenating Serum:

Reveals a smoother, evenly radiant complexion, generating a glow effect. Collagen-stimulating, dark spot brightening, and wrinkle-combatting properties restores youthful skin.Skin Concerns: Fine lines and wrinkles, rough texture, dark spots, dryness.

Hydra-Soothing Serum:

Instant, intense hydration. Effectively plumps up fine lines, reduces redness, boosts and protects the skin barrier for healthy, supple skin. Skin Concerns: Dehydration, dryness, redness, fine lines, dull skin.

Is there any aftercare that I should follow post my Micro-Infusion treatment?

After youve infused your skin with all that skin boosting serum, try to keep the skin clean for 24 hours and follow these post treatment guidelines:

  • The day after treatment, wash your face with a gentle cleanser and apply a nourishing moisturizer.
  • Users may experience minor redness and irritation after the procedure but should quickly subside. Skin may experience mild swelling, peeling or flaking. You can minimize these effects by keeping the skin moisturized.
  • Skip makeup for as long as you can, but at minimum, at least 24 hours.
  • No exercising or strenuous activity for the first 24 hours post-treatment. Sweating and gym environments are harmful, rife with bacteria, and may cause adverse reactions.
  • Keep in mind, anything you put on your skin after using Micro-Infusion will absorb mo re easily, so its important to keep the skin clean and use products that dont have harsh ingredients (no acids!).
  • Avoid touching your face, especially with dirty hands that havent been sanitized.
  • Avoid swimming, saunas and steam rooms for at least 24-48 hours post-treatment.
  • Avoid applying products containing fragrances, active ingredients or acids such as Alpha Hydroxy Acids, Beta Hydroxy Acid, Retinol (Vitamin A), Vitamin C or anything perceived as active skincare including scrubs or toners for at least 2-3 days post-treatment, as it may cause irritation.
  • Avoid exfoliating products for 2-3 days after treatment because they can be abrasive on skin when it is in that kind of condition. Avoid direct sun exposure immediately after treatment. Apply a high protection sunscreen when going outdoors, and avoid prolonged sun exposure. Use good quality non-comedogenic sunscreen if going in the sun.

By the next day, you should be free to resume your normal activities, skincare routine, and any makeup you want to applybut you may not even need it. Just ensure you monitor your skins reaction.

Q-urify Tap Water Filter

How does tap water harm my skin?

Facial skin is more sensitive than your skin anywhere else. Your skin barrier is like a protective shield against outside threats. But the stuff in tap water can harm it, leading to:

  • Dry and Damaged Skin: Tap water can strip away your skin's natural oils, leaving it dry and damaged.
  • Skin Irritation: It might also cause skin irritation, making it sensitive and red.
  • Premature Aging: The chemicals in tap water can even speed up the aging process, causing wrinkles and fine lines.

What does it filter in the water?

Q-urify water filter uses both physical and chemical filtration components to remove up to 99% of contaminants, as small as 0.2 microns, known as skin irritants that make your tap water hard like calcium, magnesium and chlorine.

What skin types can benefit from Q-urify Water Filter?

Every skin type! Especially helpful for anyone experiencing sensitive skin, premature aging, dry skin, and acne.

For best results, we recommend using the device 5-7 times a week. Each session only lasts 10 minutes. Visible results may vary, but improvements can be seen within 4 weeks of consistent use. More significant changes can be seen over several months.

How long is the treatment?

Is it safe for all skin types?

How do I charge the device?

Charge Neck & Dcolletage device fully for 4 hours before your first use. Only use a 5V adapter or computer USB port. Please avoid fast chargers as they can damage the battery.
